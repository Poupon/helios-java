Helios is a small programm which i wrote in 2001 to show briefly , the
solar system to my children.

It is placed under the GPL licence v3.

The program has three windows:

- The first present a simplified view of the solar system

- The second list celestial body, and by cliking left one of them you
  center the view on it, by clicking right, you center and  follow it
  during its trajectory.

- The third presents various regulation, as the level of zoom, the choice between the trajectory or  the orbit...
